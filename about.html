A Little About Me...

Hi, my name is Raven and I love going to restaurants. I originally started this site as a blog where I could talk about the places I like to go to eat, and then the concept took off from there. I think it's fun to plan out meal activities and since there are so many restaurants that sign up for Restaurant Week, I figured I'd make a database that arranges everything in one place. I hope you enjoy dining out as much as I do! Cheers!

If you'd like to get in touch with me, fill out the form below! I love hearing feedback and suggestions.
